Série de Fourier (cossenos e senos)


Seja $g(t)=g(t+T),\quad t\in[0,T]$, então

$$ g(t)=a_0+\sum_{n=1}^\infty a_n\cos(n\omega_0t)+b_n\sin(n\omega_0t),\quad \omega_0=\frac{2\pi}{T} $$

Base de Fourier


$$ B=\{1,\cos(n\omega_0t),\sin(n\omega_0t),\forall n\in\mathbb{N}\} $$

Base de Fourier

$$ \langle \cos(n\omega_0t), 1 \rangle = 0,\quad \langle \sin(n\omega_0t), 1 \rangle = 0 $$

Base de Fourier

$$ \langle \cos(n\omega_0t), 1 \rangle = 0,\quad \langle \sin(n\omega_0t), 1 \rangle = 0 $$

$$ \int_0^T \cos(n\omega_0t) dt = 0,\\\int_0^T \sin(n\omega_0t) dt = 0 $$

Base de Fourier

$$ \langle \cos(n\omega_0t), 1 \rangle = 0,\quad \langle \sin(n\omega_0t), 1 \rangle = 0 $$

$$ \int_{-\frac{T}{2}}^\frac{T}{2} \cos(n\omega_0t) dt = 0,\\\int_{-\frac{T}{2}}^\frac{T}{2} \sin(n\omega_0t) dt = 0 $$

Base de Fourier

$$ \langle \cos(n\omega_0t), \sin(m\omega_0t) \rangle = 0,\quad \forall n,m\in\mathbb{N} $$

Base de Fourier

$$ \langle \cos(n\omega_0t), \sin(m\omega_0t) \rangle = 0,\quad \forall n,m\in\mathbb{N} $$

$$ \cos(n\omega_0t) \sin(m\omega_0t) = \frac{1}{2}\Bigl(\sin((n+m)\omega_0t)+\sin((n-m)\omega_0t)\Bigr)\\ $$ Portanto, $$ \int_0^T \cos(n\omega_0t) \sin(m\omega_0t) dt = 0 $$

Base de Fourier

$$ \langle \cos(n\omega_0t), \sin(m\omega_0t) \rangle = 0,\quad \forall n,m\in\mathbb{N} $$

$$ \cos(n\omega_0t) \sin(m\omega_0t) = \frac{1}{2}\Bigl(\sin((n+m)\omega_0t)-\sin((n-m)\omega_0t)\Bigr)\\ $$ Portanto, $$ \int_{-\frac{T}{2}}^\frac{T}{2} \cos(n\omega_0t) \sin(m\omega_0t) dt = 0 $$
